Maria Christina Falls

Maria Christina Falls is well-known in the local place of Iligan. It's one of the tourist destinations. Though it attracts tourists Maria Christina Falls is connected to a Hydro Power Plant that provides electricity throughout the city and its neighboring cities.

Tinago Falls

Tinago Falls is one of the best falls in Iligan City. Most tourists always pick Tinago Falls as the top selection. The name "Tinago" is "Hidden" in English thus its location is very hidden or remote however, the majestic location will enchant you once you find the place.

Sikyop

Sikyop, which perfectly means "hidden paradise", is located in Sitio Lawlawon, Barangay Rogongon, Iligan City. It has various adventures from hiking, swimming, camping, and rappelling. Sikyop is a very remote place in Iligan City. Nature will capture your heart and eyes that make you want to visit the place once you leave.

Timoga Spring Pool

Timoga Spring is located in Timoga-Buru-un, Iligan City. If you want to unwind during the hot summer months. This is the top-selected pool in Iligan city. This pool is busy during the weekend however, during weekdays it has only a few people.

Paseo De Santiago

Paseo de Santiago is located in Santiago, Iligan city. This is place is near at beach. You can spend your time here with your friends or family. At night the place is busy and full of people. You can enjoy walking and dining in this place.
